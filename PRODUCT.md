# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Event organizers, marketers, and small-business operators who need to send personalized bulk email campaigns from a known contact list via Zoho Mail SMTP. Primary task: import a CSV, compose a template, and dispatch correctly personalized email to every recipient. Secondary task: confirm delivery and track failures.

## Product Purpose

MailDispatch is a self-hosted bulk email dispatch tool. It imports a CSV of contacts, merges per-row data into a subject + body template, and sends each message through Zoho Mail SMTP with built-in rate-limiting. Success means every targeted recipient receives a correctly personalized email without SMTP failures.

## Brand Personality

Reliable, precise, and direct. The tool exists to do one job and do it well. Voice: technically confident, minimal, never corporate.

## Anti-references

- Generic newsletter-platform aesthetics (editorial layout, heavy imagery, cream backgrounds)
- Cheap utility-tool aesthetics (unstyled HTML forms, no hierarchy, bare browser defaults)
- Startup-SaaS overdesign (gradient text, hero metrics, testimonial carousels, buzzword copy)

## Design Principles

1. The workflow is linear — reinforce the 4-step sequence at every touchpoint.
2. Confidence over decoration — every element earns its place by reducing uncertainty or enabling action.
3. The data is the star — contact lists, template body, delivery results all need clear visual treatment.
4. Errors surface immediately — failures are visible, specific, and actionable.
5. Speed over ceremony — users are dispatching emails, not exploring a product.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. All interactive elements keyboard-navigable. Reduced motion respected via prefers-reduced-motion. No color-only state indicators — pair with labels or icons.
